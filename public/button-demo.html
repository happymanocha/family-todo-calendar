Responsive Behavior

Resize your browser window or view on mobile to see responsive behavior:

  • Larger touch targets on mobile (48px minimum)
  • Button groups stack vertically on mobile with btn-group--mobile-stack
  • Full-width buttons adapt to container width
  • Hover effects only on devices that support hover
  • Reduced motion support for accessibility
